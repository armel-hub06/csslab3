Cartoon Watchlist

1. Avatar: The Last Airbender

The perfect fantasy adventure - Follow Aang, the last Airbender, as he masters all four elements to save a war-torn world. With rich lore, unforgettable characters, and a balance of humor/action, it's a masterpiece for all ages.

2. Gravity Falls

Mystery meets quirky humor - Twin siblings uncover supernatural secrets in a weird small town. Packed with ciphers, lovable oddballs, and an overarching mystery that pays off brilliantly.
