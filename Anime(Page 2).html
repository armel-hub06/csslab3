Anime Watchlist

1. Naruto & Naruto Shippuden

The quintessential ninja journey - Follow Naruto Uzumaki, a loud-mouthed orphan with dreams of becoming Hokage, as he trains, battles, and forms unbreakable bonds. Filled with epic fights, emotional backstories, and a sprawling world of ninjas, this is the perfect gateway anime. Skip fillers for a tighter experience.

2. Attack on Titan

Humanity's last stand against man-eating giants - A dark, action-packed masterpiece with political intrigue, moral dilemmas, and some of anime's most jaw-dropping plot twists. Eren Yeager's quest for freedom will leave you breathless.
